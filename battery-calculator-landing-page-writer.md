* Rank in Google

# Engineering Requirements

Always:

* Show calculations
* Explain assumptions
* Use realistic values
* Differentiate Ah and Wh
* Differentiate nominal and usable capacity

Never:

* Invent technical specifications
* Exaggerate runtime
* Use unrealistic battery performance
